Frequently Asked Questions

What is MarketBot?

MarketBot is a comprehensive Discord bot that transforms your server into a professional digital marketplace with secure payments, automated delivery, and enterprise-grade analytics.

Is MarketBot free to use?

Yes, MarketBot is completely free to use with basic features. We also offer Premium and Enterprise plans with advanced features for growing businesses.

What payment methods are supported?

MarketBot supports PayPal, Bitcoin, Litecoin, and CashApp payments with automated verification and secure transaction processing.

Is MarketBot secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade security with SQL injection prevention, input validation, encrypted storage, and comprehensive audit logging.

How quickly can I set up my marketplace?

Setting up your MarketBot marketplace is quick and easy. Simply add the bot to your Discord server and use the setup commands to configure your store in minutes.

Do you provide customer support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive customer support through our Discord server, documentation, and dedicated support channels for Premium users.
